08.10.20 - Learning Style

During the past couple of weeks, I have been working primarily on developing my ability to make features for the web both functional and responsive. I have come across the realisation that I love complaining to myself when my code decides not to work, I find that outwardly expressing my frustration (mindful of others, of course) helps me find solutions quicker than if I were to sit and stare at the problem for hours, talking from experience. While completing one of the tasks that my lecturer gave the class, I learnt I was a Kinaesthetic -INFJ Learner, which means that, when learning there must be some sort of physical interaction with the material. I will remember content best through touch…taste or smell? This makes sense given what I said previously. The results came to a bit of a surprise as I had not really noticed my behaviour but once I read through and answered all the quizzes questions, it was clear. If you are curious, try the quiz here.

23.10.20 - Strengths and Weaknesses

While learning flex, I struggled to understand how it worked. I did my best; however, I lean more towards grid. I believe grid to be my strength as it is easier to visualise and more comfortable to control when using media queries. Furthermore, flexbox has its limitations in terms of allowing for only one layout and you cannot make it in anything other than row and column. Again, since grid can be altered and you can target specific cells, then it is easier to create more dynamic layouts. Despite my preference for grid, I would like to become familiar with flex.
